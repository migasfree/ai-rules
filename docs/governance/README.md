# 🛡️ Governance Portal

Welcome to the **Migasfree Governance Portal**. This space centralizes the high-level oversight, architectural integrity, and adversarial assessment of our projects.

## ⚖️ The Adversarial Governance Model

We follow a **Governance Consultant** model where our Core Global Roles act as "Adversaries" to ensure maximum quality and risk mitigation. Instead of just assisting, these roles are empowered to critique the implementation from their specific domain.

### 👥 The Board of Adversaries

Every project using the Migasfree standards is overseen by these four specialized filters:

1. **[CTO (Strategic Oversight)](../../global_skills/cto_strategic_oversight/SKILL.md)**: Critiques business alignment, long-term sustainability, and systemic risk.
2. **[Technical Lead & Architect](../../global_skills/technical_lead_architect/SKILL.md)**: Critiques structural integrity, security (STRIDE), and technical debt.
3. **[Product & Experience Designer](../../global_skills/product_experience_designer/SKILL.md)**: Critiques user value, accessibility (WCAG), and cognitive load.
4. **[Solutions & Operations Lead](../../global_skills/solutions_operations_lead/SKILL.md)**: Critiques reliability, idempotency, and deployment safety.

---

## 📂 Portal Structure

- [**`/critiques/`**](./critiques/): Contains formalized critique documents and their corresponding **Defenses/Mitigations**.
- [**`/audits/`**](./audits/): Repository for generated **Strategic Audit Reports** (from `/audit_strategic`).

---

## 🔄 The Governance Workflow

1. **Discovery**: The AI agent identifies the specific stack and relevant Global Roles.
2. **Critique (Adversarial Pass)**: Each role generates a "Risk & Critique" document in `/critiques/`.
3. **Defense**: The protocol provides a formal "Counter-Argument" or "Mitigation Plan" for each point.
4. **Audit**: The `/audit_strategic` workflow synthesizes these real-world tensions into a final strategic report.
